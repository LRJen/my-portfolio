The **Digital Learning Resource Request System** is a web-based platform designed to facilitate seamless access to educational materials for students, employees, and community members. It enables users to request various digital learning resources, including PDFs, training slots, mentorship opportunities, and course enrollments, while administrators can efficiently manage, approve, or reject these requests. The system features a secure authentication process, allowing users to register and log in, with admins having additional management privileges. A user-friendly interface ensures that users can easily browse and explore available resources, while an integrated **AI-powered recommendation system** enhances the learning experience by suggesting relevant materials based on past requests and interests. For instance, a user requesting **"Java Basics"** might receive recommendations for **"Object-Oriented Programming"** or **"Web Development Basics."** Additionally, an **admin dashboard** provides essential analytics, including total requests, approvals, and the most popular resources, with visual representations such as bar charts. The system is built with scalability, security, and performance in mind, ensuring fast page loading and efficient database queries while maintaining a seamless user experience.

Smart Electric Recharge App

The Smart Electric App is an innovative mobile application designed to provide a seamless and automated way for users to recharge their prepaid electricity meters without manually entering token numbers. The app ensures a secure, fast, and user-friendly electricity recharging experience by integrating digital transactions, smart automation, and API-based meter updates. The idea behind the app originated from real-world challenges where individuals, such as homeowners and tenants, struggle with manually inputting electricity token numbers into their prepaid meters. The app eliminates this hassle by directly communicating with the meter system, ensuring that purchased electricity is automatically credited without requiring users to enter long PIN codes manually.
